About Me

I am currently a M.S. student in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University. I obtained my B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of California, Berkeley.

My focus in undergraduate was on mechatronics design. Some projects I have worked on include autonomous racing car, IR pulse oximeter and some other fun school projects. Most of the other projects I've worked on come from going to hackathons with friends.

My research interest consist on developing the technology and design techniques to advance medical devices. Some topics that I am interested in (broadly):

  • Haptic feedback in robot-assisted minimally invasive surgery (RMIS).
  • Human-robot interactions and multiple-agents collaboration in RMIS.
  • MRI-compatible tools/robots.
