About Me

I am currently a M.S. student in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University. I obtained my B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at the University of California, Berkeley.

My focus in undergraduate was on mechatronics design. Some projects I have worked on include autonomous racing car, IR pulse oximeter and an inertial measurement system. Most of the other projects I've worked on come from fun weekends in hackathons with friends.
